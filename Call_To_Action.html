So what's Finding Dory all about?

Finding Dory is an upcoming Disney-Pixar movie set sixth months after Finding Nemo. Dory's suddenly remembered a few things about her childhood, so now she's on a mission to reunite with her family off the California coast! What kinds of friends will she meet along the way? Will she ever find her missing family?
